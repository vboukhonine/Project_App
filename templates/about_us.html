About Us

Welcome to Recipe Finder, your go-to application for discovering delicious recipes tailored to your taste and dietary preferences. With a user-friendly interface, our app allows you to search for recipes by keyword, filter results by various categories, and access detailed information about each recipe. Each recipe includes a comprehensive list of ingredients, step-by-step preparation instructions, and nutritional information, ensuring you have everything you need to create a delightful meal.

Creators: Diego Valdez, Mark Roland Garban, and Vera Boukhonine

Our mission is to make meal planning easy and enjoyable by providing you with a vast collection of recipes to explore. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a cooking novice, Recipe Finder is here to inspire your culinary journey.
